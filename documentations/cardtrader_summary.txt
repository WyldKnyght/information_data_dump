The main function of the CardTrader website is to serve as a global marketplace for various trading card games, providing various game sets and categories such as singles, tokens/emblems, booster boxes, boosters, bundles and fat packs, tournament prerelease packs, complete sets, preconstructed decks, and starter decks. Additionally, the website offers features such as CardTrader Zero, 1-Day Ready, Cart Maximizer, Autopricer, MTG Print, and APIs for developers, as well as shipping, payment, refund, and FAQ support for its customers.
